T cell receptor Beta chain V region sequencing (TCR-seq)

Luning Prak Lab, University of Pennsylvania
Date revised: June 30, 2022


  1. TRB sequencing

    1. T cell receptor beta chain gene rearrangements were bulk sequenced from gDNA using similar methods to IGH sequencing. A detailed step-by-step protocol which is part of PMID: 32267929, can be found in its supplemental information:
      1. Materials and methods with amplification conditions, primer sequences and more
      2. GLIPH analysis
    2. Further details, including PCR primers, can be found in PMID: 30111633 and Meng et al; 2021 .
    3. For data analysis, we recommend using the ImmuneDB pipeline (PMID: 30298069), with database installation instructions available on GitHub.
