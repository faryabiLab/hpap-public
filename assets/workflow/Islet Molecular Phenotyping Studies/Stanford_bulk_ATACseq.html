  1. Pre- and Post- processing steps for Bulk ATACseq

    1. Please see the detailed protocol including preparation of resuspension buffer, RSBT and wash buffer as well as the ATAC reaction mix on the labs digitized SOP link here
    2. Detailed next steps can also be accessed via the above link. These next steps involve the following-
      • Permeabilization and Transposition of sorted cells
      • Elution of transposed fragments
      • Pre-amplification
      • qPCR amplification to determine number of additional cycles
      • Final amplification and cleanup
      • Library cleanup and quantification
