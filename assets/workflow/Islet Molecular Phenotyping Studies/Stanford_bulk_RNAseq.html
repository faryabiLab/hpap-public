Stanford protocol for Bulk RNAseq
(only performed for HPAP T2D donors)

Seung Kim lab, Stanford University
Date submitted: August 11, 2021

  1. Pre-processing of FACS sorted cells

    1. Spin ~20,000 FACS sorted cells at 3500 rpm or 1300 x g @ 4 ℃ for 5 minutes to pellet cells
    2. Carefully aspirate the supernatant

  2. RNA isolation using PicoPure™ RNA Isolation Kit (ThermoFisher Scientific KIT0204)

    1. Manufacturer's manual was followed to perform RNA extraction, DNase treatment, and elute in a final volume of 11 μl extraction buffer: RNA isolation kit. (If the link is broken, see local doc)
    2. Store at −80 ℃ until ready to proceed

  3. Library construction using SMARTer® Stranded Total RNA-Seq Kit v2-Pico Input Mammalian (Takara 634412)

    1. Manufacturer's manual was followed to construct library, purify with AMPure beads, and perform QC on bioanalzyer: Stranded Total RNA-Seq Kit v2. (If the link is broken, see local doc)

  4. Perform 100 bp pair-ended sequencing on Illumina HiSeq4000
