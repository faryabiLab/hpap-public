User Agreement for the HPAP Database1

    The HPAP Database is an open access database for the Human Pancreas Analysis Program (HPAP) consortia under Human Islet Research Network (HiRN). HPAP is located at the Perelman School of Medicine of the University of Pennsylvania (collectively “Penn”).  It supports a data sharing and analysis portal for Type-1 Diabetes research community and is funded by the National Institute of Health (NIH)2,3. This database contains data on Physiological phenotyping, Immunobiology assays, Advanced molecular profiling, Tissue analytics and bio-banking and Islet functional analysis.
